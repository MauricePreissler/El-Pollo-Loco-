Game Info

Controls:

Right Arrow (→): Move right
Left Arrow (←): Move left
Spacebar: Jump
Key "D": Throw bottles

Screen Controls:
Enlarge screen: Click on the diagonal arrow at the bottom center of the game screen.
Exit full screen mode: Click the cross at the top center of the screen or press the ESC key.
